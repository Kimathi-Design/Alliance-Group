## Page 18 — Properties (MDA/MRI) Integration

### Copy

**PROPERTIES (MDA/MRI) INTEGRATION**

**Integrating Property Management with Revenue Services Lesotho**

The Properties business currently operates on the MDA/MRI Property Management platform.

The proposed integration enables invoices generated within MDA/MRI to be automatically submitted to the Enterprise Compliance Hub for validation, fiscalisation and submission to Revenue Services Lesotho.

Existing property management processes remain unchanged while compliance is fully automated.

**Integration Scope**

- Tenant invoices
- Rental billing
- Service charges
- Customer validation
- QR code generation
- Credit notes
- Debit notes

**Integration Process**

1. **Invoice generated within MDA/MRI** — Property invoices are created in the existing MDA/MRI billing workflow.
2. **Invoice transmitted to Compliance Hub** — Invoice data is sent securely via API to the Enterprise Compliance Hub.
3. **Validation performed** — Customer TIN, invoice structure and mandatory fields are verified before fiscalisation.
4. **QR code generated** — An RSL-compliant QR code is produced as part of the fiscal document set.
5. **Submitted to Revenue Services Lesotho** — Validated invoices are submitted to Lekuka IDMS through Motheo.
6. **Fiscal response returned** — Acknowledgement and fiscal status are received from Revenue Services Lesotho.
7. **Invoice updated** — MDA/MRI is updated with fiscal references so records remain complete and auditable.
